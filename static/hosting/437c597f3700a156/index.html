Deep Learning Overview

What is Deep Learning?

Deep Learning is a subset of machine learning that mimics the workings of the human brain in processing data and creating patterns for use in decision making. It involves the use of neural networks with many layers (hence 'deep') to analyze various factors of data.

Applications of Deep Learning

Deep Learning has applications in a variety of fields, which include:

  • Image Recognition
  • Speech Processing
  • Autonomous Vehicles
  • Medical Diagnosis
  • Natural Language Processing

Future Prospects

The future of Deep Learning holds promise for even more sophisticated AI advancements, enabling machines to perform complex tasks with greater accuracy and efficiency.
